Devolutions

Whether you're a Nike member or purchased online with a guest account, you can ship products to us or return them at most Nike Stores. See our return instructions for more information.

You can take the products you want to return to any Nike Store, except Nike Clearance Stores.

For orders placed on Nike.com or any of the Nike Apps, have the barcode or order number ready in the store. Nike members can also provide the email or phone number used to place the order. For in-store purchases, the quickest and easiest way to return products to the store is to provide the original store receipt. If you no longer have the receipt, here are some options: If you provided your Nike membership email address or scanned your Nike Pass during checkout, a Nike Store Athlete will be able to look up your order and issue a refund to the original payment method. If you did not provide your Nike membership information and the products you want to return are unworn and unwashed, you can return them for a store credit for the current price of the product. We will also assist you with an exchange. If you return products with a gift receipt, we will provide you with store credit equal to the amount of the receipt. We can also assist you to exchange your gift.

No, products purchased from a Nike Clearance Store cannot be returned or exchanged unless they are defective or have an imperfection. Additionally, items purchased at other Nike stores or online also cannot be returned to Nike Clearance stores.

If you have not used or washed the products, we may be able to help you with the return even after 60 days. Please see our return instructions for more information.

We take responsibility for our footwear and equipment. If less than 60 days have passed since your purchase, you must choose one of the options above to return a product with defects or faults. If more than 60 days have passed, contact us to return the product. Please see our warranty information for more details.
